Check before using the estimate

Google says points are calculated from the item price and rounded to the nearest whole point for each item. Taxes do not earn points. Because the estimate treats the target as one total, actual points can differ with item prices and eligibility.

FAQ

Is one Play Point always worth $1?

No. This page estimates spending needed to earn points. Redemption value depends on the reward shown in your account.

When should I enter a promotion multiplier?

Only when the app, item, dates, activation requirement and bonus cap clearly cover the planned purchase.

Will I receive the exact result?

Not necessarily. Per-item rounding, taxes and ineligible transactions can change the total.
