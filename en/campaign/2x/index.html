FAQ

Does 2x make the cost exactly half?

Not always. It depends on base earning, status, caps, and eligible purchase amount.

Can gift cards count?

They may not count at purchase time in the way users expect. Check the local rule and compare with the gift-card guide.

Is 2x enough for Platinum or Diamond?

It depends on missing points. Use the calculator result with your current Play Points progress.
