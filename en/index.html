❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. How is the required spending calculated?

A. By entering your current status and the points needed to reach your goal status, the tool calculates the required spending based on your base points rate and campaign multiplier.

Q. What is Reverse Mode?

A. By entering the amount you plan to spend (USD), the tool simulates the estimated points you can earn based on your current status and campaign settings.

Q. Is the diary data saved?

A. Yes, but it is stored only in your browser's local storage. To prevent data loss, you can export your diary data as a JSON file or copy it, and restore it later.
