📅 Updated: 2026/06/20 ・ ⏱ 4 min read

Are you directly paying with your credit card on the Google Play Store?
Actually, that's a missed opportunity to save!
With just a little planning, you can easily get Google Play gift cards at 4% to 15% off. Saving money on your gift cards means more points and items in your favorite games! Let's explore the best ways to purchase discounted gift cards.

Tip 1: Costco Wholesale Membership Deals

If you are a Costco member, you can often buy multipacks of Google Play gift cards at a discount (usually 4% to 10% off the face value) either in-store or on their official website.

Buying in bulk from warehouse clubs is the easiest way to ensure you never pay full price for Play Store transactions.

Tip 2: Target Circle & Store Promotions

Target frequently runs promotions like "Buy one, get one 10% or 15% off" on specialty gift cards, including Google Play.

If you use a Target RedCard, you also get an automatic 5% discount on your purchases, which can stack with certain promotions. Keep an eye on the weekly ads!

Tip 3: PayPal Digital Gifts & Amazon Sales

During holiday seasons, PayPal Digital Gifts and Amazon occasionally host promotions offering $5 or $10 off a $50 Google Play gift card.

Subscribing to bargain alert sites (like Slickdeals) can help you grab these limited-time online deals instantly before they sell out.

Summary: Smart Spending = Faster Level Up!

Combine your discounted gift cards with weekend point boost campaigns to maximize your reward returns!
