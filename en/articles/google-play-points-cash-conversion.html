Can you cash out Google Play Points?

Updated July 24, 2026 · US guide

No. Google Play Points cannot be converted to cash, withdrawn to a bank, or transferred to another Google account. You can only choose eligible rewards shown under Play Points → Use, such as Play credit, coupons, and in-app or in-game items.

“Redeemable” does not mean “cashable.” A reward that works inside Google Play does not become unrestricted money.

Why Play credit is not cash

Google Play credit is a balance for eligible purchases on Google Play. It is not a checking balance, prepaid debit card, or general-purpose wallet, and it is not a route to a bank withdrawal.

Coupons can have app restrictions, minimum purchase requirements, and expiration dates. In-app items are even more specific. Compare a reward by whether you will actually use it, not only by its displayed amount.

How to check your real options

  1. Open Google Play with the account enrolled in Play Points.
  2. Select your profile, then Play Points.
  3. Open Use and review the required points, eligible app, minimum purchase, and expiration.
  4. Redeem only when the reward fits a purchase you already plan to make.

Rewards vary by country, account, and time. Treat the current Play Store screen as the deciding source.

Earning cost vs redemption value

The spend required to earn 100 points can be estimated from your level and a verified promotion. What those 100 points can unlock depends on the selected reward. There is no universal cash exchange rate that makes those two numbers equal.

Your spendable point balance and annual level progress are also separate. Redeeming points reduces the balance, but does not by itself undo the points counted toward your current-year level progress.

Avoid cash-out scams

A site or person offering a secret cash-out method is not using an official Play Points feature. Do not share passwords, verification codes, gift codes, identity documents, or remote access to your device.

Account sales and off-platform trades can lead to nonpayment, account takeover, or suspension. If an option is not shown in Play Points → Use, do not treat it as a supported redemption path.

FAQ

Can I gift my points?

No. Google does not provide an account-to-account or family transfer feature.

Can a points redemption be refunded?

Rules vary by reward, but in-app items and Play credit redeemed with points are generally not refundable. Check before confirming.

Is one point worth a fixed amount?

No. There is no universal cash rate. Compare the points required with the reward you would actually use.
