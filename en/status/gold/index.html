FAQ

Is Gold a good first target?

Often, yes. It is easier to evaluate than Platinum or Diamond because the missing amount is usually smaller.

Can I use a promotion multiplier?

Yes, but only if the exact purchase qualifies for that promotion.

Does this replace the official Google Play progress?

No. It is a planning estimate, not an account checker.
