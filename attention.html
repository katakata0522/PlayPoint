There is quite a big difference, so I’ve summarized it in the table below. Please take a look!

This comparison chart is a simple example to show how
“points needed to level up” and “points earned per currency”
can differ between Japan and other regions📝

It’s a bit surprising to see such a big gap, isn’t it? 😖
Because of this difference, this calculator uses slightly different settings
for the Japanese mode and for the English / Global mode.
I worked hard to make it useful for people all around the world!
Each mode is tuned so that it follows the rules that are commonly used
for those Play accounts as closely as possible.
I think it turned out pretty well, so show this tool some love! 😊

So, if your Google Play account is not from Japan,
please switch to the [English] tab and use the English / US (Global) mode,
instead of translating the Japanese page with auto-translate.
This will make your results much closer to the rules actually used for your account,
and help you plan your Play Points with confidence👐

To get the best and most accurate experience,
please go back to the calculator and switch the tab to "English" (US / Global mode).
